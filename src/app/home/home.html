About the app

ArticlesAngular is a place where you can share knowledge, inspire others, and develop your passions. Create, edit, and browse articles in a friendly, intuitive interface.

GitHub

Features

  • User registration and login
  • Add, edit, and delete articles
  • Search and filter content
  • Add images to articles
  • Responsive, modern interface
  • Action notifications
  • Route protection and error handling

Technologies used

Angular Angular Material Firebase AngularFire Uploadcare SCSS TypeScript Signals RxJS Playwright

How to start?

  1. Register or log in.
  2. Add and browse articles.
  3. Discover new content and develop your interests!
